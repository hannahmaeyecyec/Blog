How to Get to Panimahawa Ridge

From Cagayan de Oro

  • Ride a bus to Valencia or Davao
  • Drop off at Impalutao, Impasug-ong
  • Ride a habal-habal to Ant Farm (jump-off point)

From Malaybalay City

  • Ride a bus heading to Valencia/Davao
  • Get off at Impalutao
  • Take a motorcycle to Ant Farm

By Private Vehicle

  • Drive along Sayre Highway
  • Navigate to Ant Farm, Impalutao (commonly pinned on Google Maps)
