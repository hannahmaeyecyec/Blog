Mt. Tralala

Malaybalay, Bukidnon

By Aneca Berenguel | November 25, 2025

About

Mt. Tralala is a rising hiking destination known for its short beginner trail, open grasslands, and refreshing mountain air.

This mountain offers a gentle hike ideal for first-time climbers. Its wide summit area reveals scenic views of nearby ranges and farmland.
