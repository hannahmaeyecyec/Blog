About

Nestled in the highlands of Lantapan, Bukidnon, Mt. Kiamo offers an extraordinary blend of adventure, culture, and natural beauty. Known for its rolling hills, lush pine forests, and breathtaking scenic views, the mountain provides a peaceful escape for hikers, families, and photographers seeking a serene retreat from city life. Its moderately challenging trails make it ideal for both beginners and seasoned hikers, while the surrounding valleys and cool climate create a refreshing atmosphere perfect for camping, sightseeing, and relaxation. Reaching Palalihan Peak of Mt. Kiamo typically takes about a total of six hours (backtrail), making it possible to complete in a day hike. Hiking often commences before sunrise (around 3 am) to avoid the heat of the sun during descent. Remember to bring ample water (around 2 liters) for your hike.
