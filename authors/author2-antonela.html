Antonela E. Estrada

Mountain trekker & writer

Antonela E. Estrada was born on March 26, 2007, in Lagonglong, Misamis Oriental. Her mother is Marjory E. Estrada, and her father is Rodirik D. Estrada. She has a twin named Antoneli E. Estrada. She was raised in a caring and supportive family, where she developed a strong passion for learning and a commitment to doing her best in school. Her parents encouraged her curiosity and dedication, helping shape her into a hardworking and disciplined student.

From her elementary years, Antonela consistently demonstrated academic excellence, earning recognition as a consistent honor student. Her determination and perseverance continued throughout her schooling, culminating in her graduation from Lagonglong Senior High School as an honor student. Her achievements reflect her strong work ethic and dedication to her studies.

Antonela enjoys participating in school activities, learning new skills, and improving herself in different areas. Her commitment to learning and personal growth has helped her become a well-rounded student with a positive attitude toward challenges.

Her life is a story of perseverance, dedication, and determination. Antonela E. Estrada serves as an inspiring example of how consistent effort and hard work can lead to success. She continues to pursue her studies with determination, aiming to achieve her goals in the future.
