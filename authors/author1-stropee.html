Stropee Jeane C. Patindol

Travel Writer & Storyteller

Stropee Jeane C. Patindol, born in 2006, is an 18-year-old resident of Zone 3, Patpat, Bugcaon, Lantapan, Bukidnon. She is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Bukidnon State University, where she continues to develop her passion for learning and personal growth. As a Catholic, Stropee values faith, balance, and purpose in her everyday life.

Stropee completed her elementary education at Matlang Central School in Isabel, Leyte. She later moved to Bukidnon, where she finished her secondary education at Malaybalay City National Science High School, an environment that helped shape her academic confidence and competitive spirit.

Since Grade 7, Stropee has been active in various academic competitions, demonstrating her enthusiasm for learning and personal excellence. She has participated in journalism, extemporaneous speaking, and quiz bees, gaining valuable experience and exposure while representing her school in different events. Her involvement in these activities reflects her drive, courage, and ability to perform under pressure.

Beyond academics, Stropee has a wide range of interests. She enjoys reading books, novels, and Wattpad stories, which nurture her imagination and broaden her perspectives. She is also fond of watching anime and drama series, which provide her with entertainment and creative inspiration. Her personality is described as jolly, funny, and independent traits that make her approachable, lively, and capable of handling responsibilities on her own.

Stropee is the daughter of Jeanny Joy Comodas and Jupe Patindol, and she comes from a family with five siblings. Growing up in a big and loving household has shaped her into a resilient, adaptable, and caring individual.

Looking ahead, Stropee aspires to build a peaceful and quiet life, one grounded in stability, personal contentment, and the fulfillment of her dreams. Her goal reflects her desire for serenity and a future where she can live meaningfully and independently.
